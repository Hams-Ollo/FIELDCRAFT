# MAP INTELLIGENCE — SERHIIVKA
## WCS Conflict

> **Server:** WCS NA — Serhiivka Conflict (MA2)
> **Map type:** Urban/industrial, flat terrain with river on west edge, open agricultural east
> **Orientation:** W–E axis — **spawn sides are random each match.** Either team may hold Main Base West or the eastern positions. Push priorities below are written from a west-spawn perspective; mirror them if you spawn east.
> **Doctrine basis:** OCOKA terrain analysis; ATP 3-21.8 urban operations

---

>

## MAP OVERVIEW

Serhiivka is a **flat map with a dense urban core.** The main axis runs **W–E**, with one team pushing from the west base and the other pushing from the east. A river runs along the western edge, anchoring the west team's rear.

**FEBA (Forward Edge of Battle Area):** Typically establishes at the Super FOB / Gas Station line in the center. The team that holds Super FOB controls the map's radio relay hub and the road network that connects north and south objectives.

**Terrain type split:**
- **Northern half:** Industrial and agricultural — open fields (Wheatfield) and factory structures (Industrial Zone). Longer engagement ranges, vehicle-favorable.
- **Central corridor:** Tight urban — Gas Station, Granary, Super FOB, Apartment Complex. Predominantly CQB at 50–150m. MGs are limited by building density.
- **Southern half:** Mixed residential and open — Lakewood, Serhiivka Manor. Flanking routes through less-contested terrain.

- Apartment Complex rooftops — southern approach; covers Lakewood, the road to Serhiivka Manor

## SUPPLY ROUTES

Primary vehicle movement follows the yellow road network. Key routes:

**West spawn logistics:**
- Main Base West → Airfield → Granary → Super FOB (primary W–E supply corridor)
- Main Base West → Wheatfield → Industrial Zone (northern route; longer but avoids CQB)

**East spawn logistics:**
- Serhiivka Manor → Eastern Lumbermill → Super FOB (primary E–W supply push)
- Lumbermill → Industrial Zone (northern flanking supply line)

**Supply interdiction priority:** The road segment from Lumbermill to Super FOB is the single highest-value interdiction point. Cutting it isolates east team resupply to the entire center and north. Vehicle ambush on the eastern approach road with L-shaped formation is extremely effective here — open road, limited dismount cover.

---

## STRATEGIC PRIORITY

**West spawn push priority:**
1. **Fort Bastion** — establishes northern anchor; prevents east team from using Industrial Zone as a staging area
2. **Super FOB** — center anchor; radio coverage for all adjacent objectives
3. **Gas Station** — secures the road junction and prevents east team lateral movement
4. **Industrial Zone** — eliminates the east team's elevated observation advantage in the north
5. **Apartment Complex** — secures the southern approach; sets condition for Serhiivka Manor push

**Key decision — north or south first:** The northern route (Wheatfield → Industrial Zone → Fort Bastion) is more vehicle-favorable but exposes the west team to long-range fire in the open ground. The southern route (Airfield → Granary → Apartment Complex) is covered but CQB-heavy. **Simultaneous pressure on both prevents the enemy from shifting reserves.**

**Avoid pushing to Serhiivka Manor until Super FOB is secured** — it is deep in the east team's territory and will be reinforced before you can build a supply chain to hold it.

---

## RADIO RELAY PRIORITIES

- **Super FOB** is almost certainly the central relay node — once you hold it, both northern and southern objectives become capturable
- **Fort Bastion** likely extends relay coverage to Industrial Zone and full northern chain
- Check for unlinked objectives (no connecting line) before committing infantry — always run a relay vehicle ahead if pushing beyond Super FOB

---

## MORTAR COVERAGE NOTES

- Wheatfield and the approaches to Industrial Zone are the most effective mortar targets — open ground means full beaten-zone effect
- Super FOB can be ranged from multiple directions; a pre-registered TRP on the Super FOB center saves 30+ seconds in a firefight
- Apartment Complex interior courtyard is in dead ground from most mortar positions north of the river — forward OP required
